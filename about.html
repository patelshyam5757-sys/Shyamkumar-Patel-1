About 100SMILES Dental Care

A modern dental care facility with more than three decades of history at 33 Fantail Street in Razole, 100SMILES Dental Care combines community roots with contemporary dentistry.

Our unwavering dedication is to provide the best possible dental care for our patients. With over 50 years of experience in dental care, we pride ourselves on offering friendly, reliable and compassionate services to every patient who walks through our doors.

We offer a comprehensive range of dental services to meet the diverse needs of our clients. From routine wellness check-ups and cleanings to dental implants, orthodontics and cosmetic dentistry, our goal is to keep your smile healthy and beautiful for life.

We also place a strong focus on patient education, sharing the knowledge and resources you need to make informed decisions about your dental health — including oral hygiene tips, nutritional guidance, and support for managing dental emergencies.

Our promise to you

Care for every age

From children visiting the dentist for the first time to adults seeking restorative or cosmetic treatments, we tailor our care to your stage of life and unique needs.

Clear communication

We take time to explain diagnoses, treatment options and costs, so you always understand your choices and feel confident about your care.

Comfort & safety

With advanced technology, modern equipment and a calm, friendly environment, we aim to make every visit as comfortable and stress-free as possible.
