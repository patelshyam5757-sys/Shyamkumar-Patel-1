Our dental services

We provide preventive, restorative and cosmetic dental care for individuals and families. Below is an overview of the services you can expect when you visit 100SMILES Dental Care.

Preventive & wellness care

Regular check-ups, professional cleanings and oral hygiene education designed to prevent problems before they start and keep your smile healthy.

  • Routine wellness check-ups
  • Scale and clean appointments
  • Fluoride and preventive treatments

Restorative dentistry

Treatments to repair or replace damaged teeth, restore function and protect your long-term oral health.

  • Fillings and crowns
  • Root canal treatment
  • Dental implants and bridges

Orthodontics & cosmetic care

Options to align teeth, improve your bite and enhance the appearance of your smile.

  • Orthodontic assessments and treatments
  • Teeth whitening
  • Cosmetic dentistry and veneers

Children’s dental care

Gentle, age-appropriate care for children, including first-visit appointments and ongoing preventive support.

  • Child-friendly check-ups
  • Fissure sealants and preventive care
  • Education for kids and parents

Emergency appointments

If you experience acute pain, trauma or sudden dental concerns, contact us as soon as possible. We aim to see urgent cases promptly.

Patient education & guidance

We offer nutritional guidance, oral hygiene tips and ongoing advice tailored to your age, lifestyle and dental history.

Our new dental technologies

Staying at the forefront of dental technology helps us diagnose conditions earlier, plan with precision and deliver more comfortable treatments.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution digital imaging with lower radiation exposure compared to traditional X-rays. These scans provide clear, quick insights for accurate diagnoses.

Intraoral Cameras

Small cameras capture detailed images of your teeth and gums, enabling early detection of problems and clearer explanations of treatment plans.

Laser Dentistry

Advanced lasers support minimally invasive treatment for gum disease, cavity detection and teeth whitening with reduced discomfort and faster healing.

CAD/CAM Restorations

Our CAD/CAM system allows us to design and create restorations such as crowns, bridges and veneers in-house, often in a single visit for a precise fit.

3D Printing

3D printed models, surgical guides and dental appliances support more accurate treatment planning and improved outcomes.

Teledentistry

Secure online consultations provide convenient access to dental advice from home, ideal for follow-ups, initial assessments and general guidance.
