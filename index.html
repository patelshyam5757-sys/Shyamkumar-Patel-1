Our new dental technologies

We continuously invest in dental technology to make your visits more accurate, efficient and comfortable.

Digital X-Rays

High-resolution imaging with significantly less radiation than traditional X-rays, allowing for faster, more accurate diagnoses.

Intraoral Cameras

Detailed images of your teeth and gums help us detect issues early and explain your treatment options clearly.

Laser Dentistry

Minimally invasive gum treatments, cavity detection and whitening with reduced pain and faster healing times.

CAD/CAM Restorations

Custom crowns, bridges and veneers designed and created in-practice for a precise fit and natural appearance in as little as one visit.

3D Printing

Accurate dental models, surgical guides and custom appliances for more precise treatment planning and outcomes.

Teledentistry

Secure virtual consultations from the comfort of your home for advice, follow-ups and treatment planning.
